About The Artist

An artist shaped by decades of painting and nearly 15 years of tattoo work.

Val Gamba began painting in 1987, building a foundation in observation, composition, and expressive handmade work long before stepping into tattooing. That background continues to shape every piece with patience, character, and a strong visual point of view.

With almost 15 years of tattoo experience, the work now brings together painterly discipline, portrait influence, and custom design thinking. Each artwork is approached to feel personal, intentional, and built around the story of the person wearing or collecting it.
